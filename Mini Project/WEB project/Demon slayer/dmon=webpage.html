Japanese manga series by Koyoharu Gotōge

DEMON SLAYER

"Demon Slayer," or "Kimetsu no Yaiba," is a Japanese manga series by Koyoharu Gotōge set in the early 20th century during the Taishō era. It follows Tanjiro Kamado, a young boy whose family is slaughtered by man-eating demons. The only survivors are Tanjiro and his sister Nezuko, who, unfortunately, is slowly transforming into a demon herself. Driven by the desire to save his sister and avenge his family, Tanjiro becomes a demon slayer.
